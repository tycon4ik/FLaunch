FLaunch is a beautifully simple application launcher that puts control back in your hands. Designed for those who value efficiency and personal style, it offers a clean, unobtrusive way to access your apps — exactly when and where you need them

What Makes FLaunch Different?
Most launchers try to do everything — widgets, news feeds, weather, and more. FLaunch does the opposite. It does one thing exceptionally well: it helps you find and launch your apps in the quickest way possible.

But simple doesn't mean limited. FLaunch gives you thoughtful customization options that let you shape the launcher to fit your workflow, not the other way around.

Launch Anywhere, Anytime
Floating Bubble Trigger
A subtle, draggable bubble lives on top of your screen. Tap it — and the launcher opens instantly. No need to swipe, press home buttons, or navigate through screens. It's always there when you need it, and never gets in the way when you don't.(or map it on button via Key maper)

Focus on What Matters
Favorites First
Pin your most essential apps — like Termux, messaging, or your browser — to the dedicated Favorites section. They stay at the top, always ready for one-tap access.

Minimalist Mode
Toggle "Only Favorite Apps" to hide every other app from the main list. Suddenly, your launcher shows only what you actually use. No clutter. No noise. Just focus.

Position It Your Way
Tired of reaching across a large screen? FLaunch lets you reposition the launcher window anywhere that feels natural:

Center – classic, balanced

Bottom – perfect for one-handed use

Left or Right – docks to the side for quick thumb access

This small touch makes a huge difference in daily comfort and speed.

Express Your Style
FLaunch doesn't force a single look on you. Personalize every visual aspect:

Theme Modes – choose System (follows your device), Light, or Dark for optimal readability day or night.

Accent Colors – pick from Blue, Green, Yellow, Red, or Purple to give the interface your personal signature. (color based on wallpapers soon)

Open Animation – select how the launcher appears:

Slide Left / Right / Up

Fade in

Zoom in

Every launch feels smooth and polished, exactly the way you like it.
